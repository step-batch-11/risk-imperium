Risk

The world of global domination


About

A strategy game of world domination. Capture territories, grow your armies, and eliminate opponents to control the entire world.

Objective

Conquer all 42 territories and defeat all players.

Turn Flow

Each turn:

  1. Reinforce - Get armies (territories ÷ 3, [ min 3 ] + continent bonuses + cards)
  2. Attack - Invade adjacent territories using troops
  3. Fortify - Move armies between your connected territories (once)

Combat

  1. Attack with up to 3 troops (need 3+ army)
  2. Defender defends with up to 2 troops ( need 1+ army )
  3. Highest dice win → loser loses the number of troops they attacked/defended with
  4. In case of tie → attacker loses the number of troops they attacked with
  5. Capture territory when enemy has no army left

Cards

  1. Gain 1 card if you capture a territory
  2. Trade sets for bonus armies [ all 3 unique/distinct set of cards ]
  3. Max 5 cards → must trade [ force trade ]
  4. Eliminating a player gives you their cards
