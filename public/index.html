Risk

The world of global domination

About

A strategy game of world domination. Capture territories, grow your armies, and eliminate opponents to control the entire map.

Objective

Conquer all 42 territories and defeat all players.

Turn Flow

Each turn:

  1. Reinforce - Get armies (territories ÷ 3, min 3 + continent bonuses + cards)
  2. Attack - Invade adjacent territories using dice
  3. Fortify - Move armies between your territories (once)

Combat

  1. Attack with up to 3 dice (need 2+ armies)
  2. Defender rolls up to 2 dice
  3. Highest dice win → loser removes 1 army
  4. Capture territory when enemy reaches 0

Cards

  1. Gain 1 card if you capture a territory
  2. Trade sets for bonus armies
  3. Max 4 cards → must trade
  4. Eliminating a player gives you their cards
